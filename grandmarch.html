belle vernon's 2025 grand march

as part of yearbook club, i attended the 2025 prom grand march for belle vernon. lots of couples were photographed, so i picked a few out of the bunch to edit myself and share here.

  • camera: canon eos r100
  • edited in lightroom

some couples in this album were not notified personally about being featured here. if you are featured and would like to be removed, please contact me at dufalla@icloud.com
